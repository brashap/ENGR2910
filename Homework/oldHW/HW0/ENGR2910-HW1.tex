{\bf Example}

In the following ordinary differential equation, solve for $y(x)$,  
subject to $y(x=0) = y_{0}$, a constant:  
\[
\alpha y + \beta \frac{d y}{d x} = 0,
\] 
where, $\alpha$ and $\beta$ are constants.
